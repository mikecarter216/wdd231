Site Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel Adventures — This name was chosen because the site focuses on sustainable and eco-friendly travel experiences around the world.

Optional domain: ecotraveladventures.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inspire and guide travelers who want to explore the world responsibly. It will provide travel tips, eco-destination guides, packing advice, and a directory of eco-friendly hotels and tour operators.

Scenarios

  • Which countries have the best eco-tourism activities?
  • Some of the top countries for eco-tourism include: Costa Rica – Famous for its rainforests, national parks, and biodiversity, offering zip-lining, wildlife tours, and sustainable lodges. New Zealand – Known for its pristine landscapes, hiking trails, and conservation-focused tourism. Norway – Offers fjord tours, glacier hikes, and eco-friendly cruises. Bhutan – Focuses on “high value, low impact” tourism with strong environmental preservation laws. Kenya – Provides ethical safari experiences that support wildlife conservation and local communities.
  • How can I find affordable, eco-friendly hotels in Europe?
  • To find affordable eco-friendly accommodations in Europe: Use booking platforms like EcoHotels.com, Bookdifferent.com, or Green Pearls that filter hotels by sustainability certifications. Check for Green Key, EU Ecolabel, or EarthCheck certifications, which indicate verified environmental standards. Travel off-season to get lower rates at sustainable hotels. Consider eco-hostels and locally-run guesthouses instead of large resorts. Use Google Maps with search terms like “eco-friendly hotel” or “sustainable accommodation” in your target city.

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Navy Blue — used for headings and important titles.

Secondary Color: Orange — used for accents, highlights, and buttons.

Background Color: Light gray for the overall page background.

Typography

Headings: Orbitron — modern, futuristic feel for titles.

Body Text: Roboto — clean and easy to read for content.
